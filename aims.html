Aims

1. Reduce Violence and Conflict:

Aim to significantly reduce all forms of violence and related death rates everywhere by addressing the root causes and promoting peaceful resolutions.

2. Strengthen Institutions:

Develop effective, accountable, and transparent institutions at all levels to support the rule of law, protect human rights, and deliver essential services to all citizens.

3. Promote Human Rights:

Strengthen the protection and promotion of human rights for all individuals, regardless of race, gender, religion, or socioeconomic status, fostering an environment where everyone can thrive.

4. Cultivate a Culture of Peace:

Nurture peace as a daily practice, embedding it into the fabric of communities through education, storytelling, and art that celebrate diversity and resolve conflicts with understanding and compassion.

5. End Abuse and Exploitation:

Work towards eliminating all forms of abuse, exploitation, trafficking, and violence against children, women, and marginalized communities, ensuring their safety and dignity.

6. Empower Voices of the Vulnerable:

Amplify the voices of those who are often unheard, giving power and platform to the marginalized, ensuring that their stories and needs shape policies and decisions.

7. Strengthen Global Partnerships:

Foster international cooperation and partnerships to address global challenges such as terrorism, organized crime, and human trafficking, promoting global peace and security.

8. Protect Fundamental Freedoms:

Safeguard and promote the protection of fundamental freedoms, such as freedom of expression, association, and belief, ensuring that all individuals can live without fear of persecution or discrimination.
