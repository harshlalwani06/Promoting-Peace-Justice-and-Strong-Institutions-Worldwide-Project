Our Summary

We are specifically a dedicated non-profit organization and a group of undergraduates started this website as a source for connecting more and more people, especially youth. We are initially 8 friends together done a presentation on promoting Peace, Justice, And Strong Institutions in our college, then slowly and gradually we started a community called “WELFARE WARRIORS”. Now we are a community of around 80 youths in our area, and we all have come a long way. It has taken us about 2 years to form this community.

Our Values

Peace: We are committed to promoting non-violence and resolving conflicts through dialogue, understanding, and collaboration. Peace is not just the absence of conflict but the presence of justice and equality.

Justice: We strive for a world where everyone is treated with fairness and dignity. Our efforts focus on fighting against systemic inequalities and ensuring that justice is accessible to all.

Strong Institutions: We support the development and strengthening of institutions that are transparent, accountable, and inclusive. Strong institutions are the backbone of a just and peaceful society.
